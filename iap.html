What is Intelligent Advisory Portfolio (IAP)?

Understanding IAP with simple explanations and real-world comparisons

Intelligent Advisory Portfolios (IAP) are pre-designed innovative investment solutions, curated to simplify equity market investing for those who don't have time to actively manage investments. IAPs are diverse range of pre-packaged equity portfolios curated by Registered Investment Advisors (RIAs).

These portfolios are curated & monitored by RIAs and follow strict rules & parameters to manage the risk-return ratio of your investment. Perfect for both active and passive investors seeking professional portfolio management.

Simple Analogy: The Personal Chef

Imagine you want to eat healthy meals but don't have time to plan, shop, or cook. You hire a personal chef who creates a weekly meal plan based on your dietary preferences, health goals, and taste preferences.

The chef monitors your health progress, adjusts recipes when needed, and ensures you get balanced nutrition. You just need to tell them your budget and preferences - they handle everything else.

Similarly with IAP:

Instead of a chef, you have RIA experts who create and monitor investment portfolios based on your financial goals, risk appetite, and investment horizon - you just invest and relax.
